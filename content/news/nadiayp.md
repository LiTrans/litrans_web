Nadia Bisek was nationally selected to participate in the 2025 Canadian Urban Transit Association (CUTA) Young Leaders Summit, a premier youth conference dedicated to cultivating the next generation of urban mobility and transit professionals. Hosted in Montreal, the summit brought together emerging leaders from across Canada to engage in critical discussions on the future of transit, sustainability, and inclusive urban design.
