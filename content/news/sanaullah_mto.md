Irum completed her Ph.D. in Intelligent Transportation Systems from [Loughborough University](https://www.lboro.ac.uk/), UK, in 2013. She has been working in different research areas including road safety, autonomous vehicles, GPS data and travel time estimation, and sustainable transportation. She is also teaching Transportation subjects at the [University of Toronto](https://www.utoronto.ca/).
